Bitnation APP

Documents are essential to any aspect of our day to day life, and in a globalized interconnected world, traditional means of bureaucracy do not suffice. Current systems are either slow, insecure, or both.

Bitnation app offers a way to issue, manage and verifies documents in a peer-to-peer manner while the Bitnation eID is a digital ID that hosts documents attached to it. The private data is hosted on the user’s device and the validity of the data is provided by notarization on the Pangea Blockchain.
